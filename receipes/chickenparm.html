Chicken parmesan

Description

Chicken parmesan a chicken cutlet fried in breading mixed with parmesan with sauce and mozzarella melted on top a delicious and quick meal to make with minimal ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless boneless chicken breast halves
  • salt and black pepper to taste
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup of panko bread crumbs
  • 3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese, divided
  • 2 tablespoons of all purpose flour, or more if needed
  • 1/2 cup of olive oil for frying, more if needed
  • 1/2 cup prepared tomato sauce of your choice
  • 1/4 cup of fresh mozzarella, cu tinto small cubes
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1/2 cup grated provolone cheese
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil

Steps to make chicken parmesan

  1. prehead oven to 450 degress farenheit
  2. place chicken breast between two sheet of plastic on a solid surface and firmly pound the chicken until it is a thickness of 1/2 inch
  3. season chicken with salt and pepper. sprinkle flour over chicken breast even coating both sides
  4. beat eggs in a shallow bowl and set aside
  5. mix breadcrumbs and 1/2 cup of parmesan cheese in a separte bowl and set aside
  6. dip a flour coated chicken breast in beaten eggs then move it the bread crumb mixture coating both sides. repeat for each chicken breast
  7. heat olive oil in a large skillet on medium-high heat until it begins to shimmer. cook the chicken in the oil until golden, about 2 minutes per side. The chicken will finish cooking in the oven.
  8. transfer the chicken to a baking dish. top each piece of chicken with 2 tablespoons of tomato sauce. layer each chicken breast with equal amount of mozzarella cheese, fresh basil and provolone cheese. sprinkle remaining parmesan over top. drizzel each piece of chicken with 1/2 teaspoon of olive oil
  9. Bake in the preheated oven until the cheese is brown and bubbly and chicken is atleast 165 degrees farenheit in the center
