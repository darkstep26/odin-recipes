Carbonara

Description

Carbonara is a simple italian dish that uses guanciale, eggs, pecorino romano cheese spaghetti and black pepper to create a deliscous quick dinner

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • 1 pound Guanciale diced
  • 16 ounces Spaghetti
  • 3 large eggs
  • 10 tablespoons grated pecorino romano cheese, divded
  • salt and freshly ground pepper to taste

Steps to make Carbonara

  1. heat olive oil in skillet over medium heat, add guanciale cook until browned then drain on paper towels
  2. bring large pot of salted water to a boil. cook spaghetti until tender but firm to the bite, about 9 minutes. keep about half a cup of pasta water then drain pasta
  3. whisk eggs 1/2 of pecorino romano cheese and some black pepper in a bowl until smooth and creamy
  4. pour egg mixture of pasta, stirring quickly so the eggs do not cook until a creamy sauce occurs, you can pour a little pasta water if it is too dry. stir in guanciale
  5. top with reamining peocrino romano cheese and salt and pepper to taste
